About Categories

Categories help you organize your income and expenses for better financial tracking:

  • Default categories are pre-configured for your convenience.
  • You can edit any category to change its name, color, or icon.
  • Categories marked as Default were created automatically but can be customized.
  • When a category is used in transactions, deleting it will archive it instead of removing it completely.
